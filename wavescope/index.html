Wavescope

A manifestation of waves in light.

This light installation tries to visually explore and expose the otherwise invisible reality of waves, be they mechanical or electromagnetic. In some measure sparked by the current controversy of the 5G broadband network, it endeavors to bring to light this imperceptible phenomenon and make it a bit more tangible and perhaps evoke a novel experience and a sense of perception in the viewer.
